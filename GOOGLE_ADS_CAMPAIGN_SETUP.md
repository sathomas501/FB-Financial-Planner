## Campaign Monitoring & Optimization

### First 24 Hours:

**Check:**
- Are ads approved? (Status should be "Eligible")
- Are you getting impressions?
- Are you getting clicks?
- Check Quality Score after 1 day

**If no impressions:**
- Increase bids (try $4-6 max CPC)
- Check if budget is limited
- Verify keywords have search volume

---

### First Week:

**Daily tasks:**
- Check spend vs budget
- Review search terms (add negatives if irrelevant)
- Check conversion tracking (GA4 events firing?)
- Adjust bids based on performance

**What to look for:**
- CTR > 2% (good for search)
- Quality Score > 5/10
- Any conversions yet? (may take time)

---

### First Month:

**Weekly tasks:**
- Review search terms report
- Add negative keywords
- Pause underperforming ad variations
- Test new headlines/descriptions
- Adjust budgets based on performance

**Optimization:**
- Campaigns with conversions: Increase budget 20%
- Campaigns with no conversions: Review keywords, ads, landing pages
- High-cost keywords with no conversions: Pause or lower bids

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Issue: Ads say "Limited by budget"

**Solution:** Increase daily budget OR lower max CPC bids

### Issue: Low impressions

**Solution:**
- Increase max CPC bids
- Add more keywords (broader match)
- Expand geographic targeting

### Issue: High impressions, low clicks (low CTR)

**Solution:**
- Improve ad copy (test new headlines)
- Add more specific keywords
- Use negative keywords to filter out irrelevant searches

### Issue: High clicks, no conversions

**Solution:**
- Check landing page (is CTA clear?)
- Verify conversion tracking (test with GCLID)
- Review search terms (are you attracting wrong audience?)
- Check mobile experience (most traffic is mobile)
