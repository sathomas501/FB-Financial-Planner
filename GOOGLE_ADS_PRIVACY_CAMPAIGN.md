# Google Ads Privacy Campaign

Privacy-focused Google Ads assets for Fatboy Financial Planner, built around the current product positioning:

- Local-first by default
- Your data stays on your device
- No account linking required
- Optional encrypted cloud sync
- Serious retirement and tax planning tools

Use privacy as a trust and differentiation layer, not the only promise. The strongest angle is:

`Serious retirement planning` + `privacy-first design` + `no subscription / no account linking`

---

## Recommended Campaign

**Campaign Name:** `Privacy-Focused Retirement Planning`

**Goal:** Drive high-intent traffic from people who care about privacy, desktop software, local-first workflows, and avoiding bank/account linking.

**Recommended Landing Page:**
- Homepage or a dedicated privacy landing page
- If using an existing page, make sure the hero clearly states:
  - Your data stays on your device
  - No account linking required
  - Optional encrypted sync

## Messaging Rules

### Lead With Capability

The user is still shopping for outcomes, not architecture. Lead with:

- Serious retirement planning
- Tax and Roth modeling
- Monte Carlo and scenario testing

Then reinforce with privacy:

- Your data stays yours
- No account linking
- Optional encrypted sync

### Avoid Weak Positioning

Avoid making the campaign sound like:

- A generic budgeting app
- A cybersecurity product
- A privacy tool without planning depth

This campaign should feel like a serious planning tool that also happens to respect user privacy.
