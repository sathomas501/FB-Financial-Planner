Privacy by Design

Your financial data belongs to you.

Most retirement software sends your income, assets, and account details to a cloud server. That data is used for analytics, shared with marketing partners, and potentially used to train AI models.

Fatboy is built differently. The desktop app runs entirely on your machine — no cloud sync, no account creation, no data harvesting. The web app runs Python locally in your browser via Pyodide — there's no server receiving your numbers.
