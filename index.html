Privacy by Design

Your financial data belongs to you.

Most retirement software sends your income, assets, and account details to a cloud server. That data is used for analytics, shared with marketing partners, and potentially used to train AI models.

Fatboy is built differently. The desktop app runs entirely on your machine — no account creation, no data harvesting. The web app runs Python locally in your browser via Pyodide — your calculations never touch a server.

Pro users can optionally sync an encrypted backup across devices. Your plan is encrypted in your browser before it leaves — using your license key as the encryption key. We receive ciphertext we cannot read. Your retirement plan is nobody else's business.

🖥
Desktop: fully local

The Windows and Linux apps run entirely on your machine. No internet required for planning. Data lives only on your computer.

🌐
Web app: Pyodide in-browser

The web app runs Python locally in your browser — not on a server. Your numbers stay on your device by default. No account required for the trial.

🔑
Encrypted cloud sync (optional)

The only automatic server call is license key validation. Pro users can optionally sync plans across devices — but the data is encrypted in your browser before upload, using a key only you hold. We store ciphertext we cannot read. How it works →
