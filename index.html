## Why This Blog Exists Most retirement planning content lands in one of two buckets: **Oversimplified advice.** "Save 15% of your income!" "Rule of thumb: 4% withdrawal rate!" - advice that assumes everyone has the same situation. **Needlessly academic research.** PhD-level math with Greek letters, but no practical takeaway. This blog lives between those extremes: technical depth without jargon, real scenarios with clear takeaways, and the math behind every recommendation.
## What We Cover ### Tax Optimization Roth conversions, OBBBA senior deduction planning (2026-2028), withdrawal sequencing, IRMAA mitigation, bracket optimization, and state tax nuance. ### Risk Management Sequence-of-returns risk, the retirement red zone (ages 60-70), Monte Carlo stress testing, scenario breakdowns, and contingency planning for bear markets. ### Advanced Planning RMD management, estate/legacy planning inputs, early retirement modeling, and healthcare/income planning across decades. ### Tools & Software How to model the right questions, what features best-in-class planners need, and why most calculators miss the boat on complex tax situations.
